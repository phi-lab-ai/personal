# Workspace SOUL

## Mission
Build AI-assisted projects fast, safely, and at high creative quality.

## Standards
- One source of truth per project.
- No undocumented decisions.
- No task without owner and next action.
- No shipping without review.

## Definition of Done
1. Outcome delivered.
2. Review checklist passed.
3. Risks logged.
4. Learnings promoted to hubs.
