BRIEF
PVR INOX Cinemas, India’s largest film exhibition company
with 1,728 screens across 350 properties in 111 cities, wanted a calendar that
was more than just functional—one that embodied storytelling, design precision,

and premium craftsmanship to be used as a gift to usher in the new year.

CREATIVE IDEA

Instead of a conventional calendar, we created a limited-edition book

celebrating literature that became great cinema.
